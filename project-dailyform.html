About the project
The Daily Form is a curated platform for design objects — things that are lovely, useful, and worthy of attention. Florencia Souto and Nano Armanini built a brand language that feels playful yet intentional: a distinctive logotype with personality, illustrated mascots that define each product category, warm editorial photography, and packaging that becomes part of the unboxing ritual. The result is a brand that makes people fall in love at first sight.
