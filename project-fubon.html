About the project
MixCode crafted a complete visual identity and motion system for the Fubon Guardians 2025 season. The campaign spans animated player cards, opening title sequences, broadcast lower-thirds, and social media content — all united by a bold typographic language built on Coolvetica, a palette of electric blue, hot pink, and gold, and a kinetic energy that mirrors the speed and spirit of baseball.
