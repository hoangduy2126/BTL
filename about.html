01 — About Us

We craft identities that outlast the moment they're made.

VRTX Studio is an independent design studio operating at the intersection of strategy and craft. We believe a brand is more than a visual system — it's a living argument for why something should exist in the world.

Founded in 2026 by 5 members who are gifted students of VNU-UET, we've partnered with founders, cultural institutions, and global companies to define the visual languages that shape how people perceive the things they love.

Our process is deliberate and unhurried. We don't arrive at a logo on day one. We arrive at understanding first — of your audience, your competitors, your ambition — and let the form follow from there.

The result is work that holds. Work that looks right in five years. Work with a reason behind every decision.
