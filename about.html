01 — About Us

We craft identities that outlast the moment they're made.

VRTX Studio is an independent design studio operating at the intersection of strategy and craft. We believe a brand is more than a visual system — it's a living argument for why something should exist in the world.

Founded in 2019, we've partnered with founders, cultural institutions, and global companies to define the visual languages that shape how people perceive the things they love.

Our process is deliberate and unhurried. We don't arrive at a logo on day one. We arrive at understanding first — of your audience, your competitors, your ambition — and let the form follow from there.

The result is work that holds. Work that looks right in five years. Work with a reason behind every decision.

What we stand for
01
Intention over impulse
Every decision has a reason. We don't reach for a typeface because it looks good — we reach for it because it says the right thing.
02
Depth over decoration
Surface beauty fades. We design systems that carry meaning at every level — from the mark to the motion to the copy voice.
03
Collaboration as craft
The best work happens when client and studio think together. We treat every project as a partnership, not a transaction.
04
Longevity by design
We design for the long arc. A VRTX identity should feel as contemporary in a decade as it does the day it launches.
